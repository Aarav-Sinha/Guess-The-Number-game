SUPPORT FAQ

Q: How is the High Score calculated?

A: The High Score tracks your **fewest guesses** to successfully crack the code. Lower is better!

Q: Why did I lose my Win Streak?

A: Your streak resets to zero if you fail to guess the code within the allotted attempts or if you manually click the RESET button.

Q: Is the secret code truly random?

A: Yes. The system uses a standard JavaScript function to generate a cryptographically weak (but practically random) number between 1 and 100.
